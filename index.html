Tena Koe, welcome to my portfolio website!

My name is Ella Wipatene, and I am a third-year Software Engineering student, with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence at Victoria University.

From the very beginning of my academic journey, I've been captivated by the boundless possibilities that technology offers, particularly when it comes to addressing environmental challenges and preserving Maori culture.

On this website, I look forward to showcasing some of my design and technology projects. Thank you for visiting!

Design

Hoahoa

Ever since high school, I have had a passion for design, which has remained a constant in my life. My journey in the world of design has been both rewarding and fulfilling. In 2020, I was honored to receive the prestigious NZQA Top in Design award, for my work on my Parihaka Convention Center branding.

While pursuing my degree in Software Engineering at university, I have found innovative ways to incorporate my design skills into my academic work. Design is an integral part how I approach problem-solving and creativity in every aspect of my life.
